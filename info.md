# information 

Bilal Family and Multicuisine Restaurant
Bilal Multi-cuisine Restaurant in Ongole offers a diverse menu featuring North Indian, Chinese, Arabian, and Seafood cuisines, with a strong emphasis on biryanis and family packs. 

Key Menu Categories & Highlights
Biryani & Rice

Non-Veg Biryanis: Bilal Chicken Fry Biryani, Hyderabadi Chicken Dum Biriyani, Mutton Dum Biryani, Special Chicken Biryani, Mutton Fry Biryani, Prawns Biryani, Fish Biryani, and Chicken Tikka Biriyani. 
Veg Biryanis: Paneer Biryani, Kaju Biryani, Mushroom Biryani, and Vegetable Pulao. 
Mandi & Platters

Mandi: Chicken Fry Mandi, Chicken Mandi Juicy, Mutton Fry Mandi, and Mixed Mandi (available in single and family portions). 
Platters: Mixed Grilled Platter, Chicken Platter, Chinese Mix Platter, Mutton Mix Platter, and Seafood Platter. 
Starters & Main Courses

Chicken Starters: Tandoori Chicken, Bilal Chicken Fry, Chicken Lollypop, Chilly Chicken, Chicken 65, and various kebabs.
Mutton Curries: Mutton Kheema Curry, Dum Ka Mutton, Gongura Mutton, Matka Mutton, and Mutton Rogan Josh. 
Seafood: Prawns Fry, Fish Dry Masala, Goan Fish Masala, and starters like Appollo Fish and Tandoori Prawns.
Indian Breads: Tandoori Paratha, Butter Naan, Plain Roti, and Assorted Bread Basket. 
Desserts & Beverages

Desserts: Gulab Jamun with Ice Cream, Qurbani Ka Meetha, Kunafa, and Bilal Special Ice Cream. 
Drinks: Mango Lassi, Butter Scotch Milkshake, Virgin Mojito, and various mocktails like Blue Hawain. 
Restaurant Details
Location: Shop 1, Municipal Complex, Addanki Bus Stand, Ongole Locality, Ongole. 
Price Range: Approximately ₹500 for two. 
Rating: 4.0/5 stars based on over 1,200 ratings.
Hours: Open daily from 11:00 AM to 11:00 PM (closes at midnight on some days per some listings).

Key Highlights for Your Website

Service Efficiency: The service is consistently described as fast and friendly. This is a major selling point you can use to highlight it as a reliable spot for both a quick lunch and a relaxed dinner.
